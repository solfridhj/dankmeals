Bread Soup
INGREDIENTS

portions
120
g old bread, in pieces
2
tablespoons of butter
1
chopped onion
8
dl chicken bouillon
1
boat of chopped garlic
400
g frozen peas
0.5
teaspoons salt
0.5
teaspoons pepper
Bread soup
  1. Take out frozen peas from the freezer, and let them defrost on the counter.
  2. Chop onion. Cut the crust of the bread, and slice it up into cubes.
  3. Melt butter in a casserole, and fry the onion in it until its soft.
  4. Pour 3/4 of the bouillon in the casserole, and boil. Let it boil for 3-4 minutes. Add the peas and bread.
  5. Mash all the ingredients together.
  6. Pour in more bouillon if the soup is too thick. Add salt and pepper.
