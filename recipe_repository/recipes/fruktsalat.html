Fruit Salad
INGREDIENTS

portions
1
vanilla bean
3
dl water
50
g sugar
2
g oranges
1
pack green grapes(500 g)
200
g strawberry
1
banana
2
red apples
Fruit Salad
  1. Cut the vanilla bean and scrape out the seeds with a knife. Boil water, sugar and vanilla bean with seeds. Let it cool down.
  2. Use a sharp knife and cut the top and bottom of the orange. Peel the rest of the orange. Put the orange in a bowl.
  3. Cut grapes in two, strawberries in four. Cut the banana in pieces and the apple in boats. Put the fruit in a bowl with the orange and mix all together.
  4. Poor over the sugar water and let the fruit absorb some of the sugar water before serving.
