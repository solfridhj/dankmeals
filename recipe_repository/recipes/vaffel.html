0.5

65
g cashew nuts
Sweet Potato Waffle
  1. Grate the sweet potatoes.
  2. Whip the eggs, and mix them with grated sweet potato, choped garlic, spring onion and flour. Add some salt and pepper.
  3. Fry the wafles, and let them cool on a grating.
  4. Fry cashew nuts in a dry pan until golden. Chop them.
  5. Put greek yoghurt, chili oil, coriander and cashew nuts on top of the waffles.
