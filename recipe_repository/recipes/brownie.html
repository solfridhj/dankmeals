  2. Melt the butter in a pot, then let it cool.
  3. Whip eggs until they are frothy.
  4. Mix the cake mix, cocoa and vanilla sugar together. Mix slowly.
  5. Put in the butter and mix slowly. Transfer the mixture over to the roasting pan.
