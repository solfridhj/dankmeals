INGREDIENTS

portions
500
g flour
0.5
packs yeast
2
ts salt
0.5
liters of water
1
dl oliveoil
3
crushed cloves of garlic
Foccacia
  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt and water. Kned the dough.
  2. Let the dough rise for 30 minutes.
  3. Pour into a pan, with paper in the bottom. Pour oil over the dough, and the garlic colves.
  4. Add salt, and spices.
  5. Cover the dough, and let it rise for an hour
  6. Let it bake in the oven at 250 degrees for 25 minutes.
