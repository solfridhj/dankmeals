Burger in whole wheat tortillas
  1. Clean the onion and cut in thin rings. Cut the avocado and remove the stone.
  2. Mix the salt, pepper and water with the minced meat. Form burgers and put on oil. Grill the burgers for 4-5 minutes on each side, and grill the avocado slices for 1 minute.
  3. Heat the tortillas some seconds on the grill, and fill with burger, grilled avocado, red onion and some ruccula.
