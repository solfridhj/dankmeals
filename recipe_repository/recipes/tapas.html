INGREDIENTS

portions
1
dl aioli
10
meatballs (pre-made)
8
dates
8
pieces of bacon
10
pieces of Foccacia
1
dl salt
16
small potatoes
Tapas
  1. Fry the meatballs
  2. Put one bacon piece around each date, and bake in the oven for 10 minutes
  3. Boil the potatoes with 5 dl of water with 1 dl salt for 20 minutes.
  4. Remove the potatoes from the water and serve
